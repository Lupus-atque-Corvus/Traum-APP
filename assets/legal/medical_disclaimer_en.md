# Medical Disclaimer

TRAUM is **not a medical device** within the meaning of EU Regulation 2017/745 (MDR).

## What TRAUM is
A personal tracking and information tool.

## What TRAUM is not
- No medical diagnosis
- No therapy recommendation
- No substitute for medical advice

## Health notes
All values calculated in the app (health score, water goal, etc.)
are based on general scientific studies and are designed for
healthy adults. They do not replace individual
medical assessment.

## In an emergency
**Europe/Germany:** 112
**USA/Canada:** 911
**UK:** 999

In case of acute health problems, immediately contact
emergency services or a doctor.
