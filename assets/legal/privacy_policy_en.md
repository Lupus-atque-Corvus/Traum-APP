# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025

## Controller
TRAUM App · Contact: support@traum-app.de

## What data we process
TRAUM stores data exclusively locally on your device:
- Profile data (name, age, weight, height)
- Health and fitness data (training, sleep, steps, heart rate)
- Nutrition data (meals, calories, water)
- Financial data (budget, transactions)
- Medication and supplement data
- Cycle data (only if enabled)

## Where data is stored
**All data remains exclusively on your device.** There is no server,
no cloud, no transfer to third parties.

## External services
- **OpenMeteo API:** For weather, coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  are transmitted to api.open-meteo.com. No personal data.
- **Health Connect / Apple Health:** Health data is retrieved from the
  device manufacturer's platform. Their privacy policies apply.
- **GitHub:** For app updates, the GitHub API is queried (no user data transmitted).

## Your rights (GDPR)
- **Access:** All data is visible in the app
- **Correction:** All fields are editable
- **Deletion:** Settings → Delete data
- **Portability:** Settings → Export data (JSON/CSV)
- **Withdrawal:** Consent can be withdrawn at any time (leads to data deletion)

## Special categories of data
Health data is specially protected under GDPR Art. 9.
You expressly consent to their processing (Art. 9 para. 2 lit. a).
Processing takes place exclusively locally on your device.

## Complaints
Competent supervisory authority: Data Protection Commissioner of your country.
